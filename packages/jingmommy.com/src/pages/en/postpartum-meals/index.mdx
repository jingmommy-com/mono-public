## Give Yourself the Best Care During Confinement

The postpartum period is both joyful and full of challenges. Your body needs adequate nutrition to recover, and more time to adjust to a new rhythm of life. When your baby arrives, your body is going through a profound transformation. During this time, food is not just about filling your stomach — it directly affects your recovery speed, energy levels, and even how smoothly breastfeeding goes.

### 1. Save Time and Effort — Focus on Rest and Your Baby

With all the postpartum body changes and newborn care, every minute is precious. Our postpartum meals require no prep or planning — simply heat and enjoy. Let us take care of all three meals so you can focus on healing and bonding with your baby.

### 2. Balanced Nutrition to Support Rapid Recovery

The postpartum confinement period is your body's golden window for repair. Every cell is regenerating. High-quality protein, collagen, and warming tonic ingredients are key to recovery. Our meals are carefully designed for each stage of postpartum recovery — from detox, to tissue repair, to deep nourishment — without excessive seasoning or over-supplementing, so you recover steadily and comfortably.

### 3. Delicious and Suited to Modern Moms' Tastes

Our meals are light, not greasy, blending Eastern and Western nutritional philosophy and cooking techniques. Diverse cooking methods — steaming, braising, slow cooking, roasting — mean nourishing food also tastes wonderful. Every dish is thoughtfully crafted to preserve the natural nutrition and flavor of fresh ingredients.

Whether you choose the 30-day full-recovery Herbal or Classic Meals, the 15-day flexible Easy Meals, the budget-friendly Vita Meals, or the Low-Carb Meals for rapid body restoration — we have the right plan for you.

Postpartum dietary support should follow a "phased" approach. Without the right method, indiscriminate over-supplementing can lead to excess weight gain and make it hard to regain your figure — it can also interfere with uterine contraction and the clearing of lochia. During this phase, "appropriate" is the guiding principle. Through phased nourishment, your body's physiological functions gradually restore. Follow the principle of clearing lochia first, then replenishing qi and blood — proper nourishment avoids weight gain and helps you make the most of this golden opportunity to transform your constitution.

| Week | Focus |
|------|-------|
| Week 1 | Replenish blood, expel lochia, restore uterine function |
| Week 2 | Promote metabolism, restore stamina, prevent postpartum back pain |
| Weeks 3–4 | Deep replenishment of qi and blood, strengthen constitution, anti-aging, boost immunity |

### Week 1: Gentle Replenishment

<span class="text-amber-900">The dietary focus in Week 1 is on clearing lochia and strengthening kidney detoxification function</span>, with pork liver as the primary blood-building food.

Because the postpartum constitution is one of "deficiency with stagnation," heavy supplementation is not appropriate. Instead, Week 1 focuses on promoting perspiration and helping the body eliminate excess fluids, enhancing metabolism. Take advantage of the Week 1 "postpartum diuretic period" to make the most of your golden window for postpartum weight loss!

### Week 2: Warming Nourishment

<span class="text-amber-900">The dietary focus in Week 2 is on preventing back and joint pain, and continuing to support uterine contraction and lochia clearance</span>, with sesame oil kidney as the primary dish, supplemented by Du Zhong herbal tonic. This uses bone- and sinew-strengthening foods to restore the joints and muscles loosened by pregnancy and childbirth.

### Weeks 3 & 4: Deep Replenishment

<span class="text-amber-900">By Weeks 3 and 4, most physiological functions have largely improved and lochia has mostly cleared</span> — this is the optimal time for deep nourishment. The dietary focus shifts to addressing qi deficiency, building stamina, anti-aging, and replenishing vital energy. This stage centers on delicious and nourishing sesame oil chicken — flavorful chicken with rich, warming broth that warms the body from within.

**A complete 30-day phased postpartum recovery** is not just about supplementation — it is about **repairing damage, rebuilding strength, and strengthening your constitution**. The result: with each birth, you grow healthier and more radiant!
