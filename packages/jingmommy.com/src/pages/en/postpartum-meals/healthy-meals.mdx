**For those who want to eat with peace of mind every day — no more stressing about three meals.**

In a busy life, it's not that you don't have time to eat — it's that **you don't have time to cook, and you're tired of settling**. We often choose takeout or restaurant meals for convenience. But eating out tends to be too greasy and too salty, and before you know it, your body feels heavier and less comfortable. What you really want is simple — someone to prepare **a perfectly portioned, freshly cooked hot meal** for you every day, looking after your health so you don't have to think about three meals.

We understand this frustration, and we know you're not overly picky — you just have basic expectations for **what goes into your body: safe, healthy, and just right.**

**Fresh-cooked and delivered same day — not frozen food**

Every Jingmommy Healthy Meal dish is freshly prepared each morning by our dedicated team, delivered directly from our central kitchen to your door. Even on your busiest days, you can enjoy a warm, home-style cooked meal.

**Less oil, less salt — eat with real peace of mind**

We don't aim for bland and tasteless — we aim for "seasoned just right": fragrant but not heavy; flavorful but not harsh. Made with gentle ingredients and warming cooking methods, suitable for seniors, pregnant women, and working adults alike — no dry mouth or digestive discomfort.

**No need to plan your own menu — no unpleasant surprises**

Just leave it to us every day. Our meal combinations are nutritionally balanced and varied, so you never have to worry about what to eat today.

---

**Who is this for?**

- Office workers who sit for long hours, have busy schedules, and are tired of eating poorly
- Expectant mothers who are pregnant and especially careful about ingredients
- Seniors with a sensitive stomach, dental concerns, or a preference for warm cooked food
