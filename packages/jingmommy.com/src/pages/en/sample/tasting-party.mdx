#### Event Details

- Two sessions per event: 12:15 PM (Chinese session) / 11:00 AM (English session). Each session is approximately 45 minutes.
- Moms who sign up on-site receive a complimentary gift — while supplies last.
- The tasting party is completely **free**, but <span class="text-pink-700 font-bold">spots are limited</span> — advance registration is required for entry.
- Current-month parties <b>give priority to moms with a due date within 3 months</b> (moms with later due dates are welcome to register and will be placed on a waitlist as needed).
- Please limit attendance to <b>two adults</b> (let us know in advance if you are bringing children).
- If you need to cancel or reschedule, please notify us in advance so we can open your spot to other moms — thank you for your understanding!
- To maintain event quality, a no-show fee of $20 per person may be charged for unexcused absences.
