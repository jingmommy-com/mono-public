### Ingredients

The primary ingredient in Jingmommy's Back Relief Supplement is Du Zhong (Eucommia ulmoides Oliver), the dried bark of the Eucommia plant — a precious Chinese tonic herb. It nourishes the liver and kidneys and strengthens the tendons. As recorded in the Shennong Bencao Jing (Divine Farmer's Classic of Materia Medica), Du Zhong "treats lumbar and knee pain, supplements the middle, benefits essential qi, strengthens tendons and bones, eliminates dampness and itching below, and resolves urinary dribbling. With long-term use, it lightens the body and promotes longevity."

#### Recommended Timing for Postpartum Mothers

Jingmommy's Back Relief Supplement is recommended starting from the second week postpartum. Take after three meals daily, 2 capsules each time.  
Moms experiencing frequent back pain may add an additional dose before bedtime and adjust based on recovery progress.

#### How Long Should I Take It?

We recommend that new mothers continue taking it through the end of the confinement period. It can also be used as a regular daily supplement thereafter.
