Clear, nourishing, and full of restorative power

Sea bass is rich in high-quality protein and a variety of amino acids. Traditional Chinese medicine considers it neutral in nature with a sweet flavor, capable of tonifying qi, strengthening the spleen, promoting wound healing, supporting tissue repair, and boosting immunity. Low in fat, low in cholesterol, and easy to digest and absorb — especially suited for recovery after childbirth, surgery, or illness.

The broth uses fresh sea bass paired with gentle herbs — clear and fresh without any fishiness. It nourishes without causing dryness or digestive burden. Jingmommy flash-freezes to lock in nutrition and freshness — just open and heat, no preservatives, handcrafted with care. Suitable for gentle postpartum and post-surgical recovery, or for anyone who wants a light, nutritious everyday soup.
